What is Treatment Protocol?

Our weight loss management program targets two things that are commonly looked over in normal diets. Hormone regulation and appetite control. This treatment utilizes a class of medicinal injections, incretin mimetics, modeled after the naturally occurring GLP-1 analogs found in your body. GLP-1 regulates how much insulin is released as well as regulates appetite. Ultimately, the injections will help the pancreas release the right amount of insulin as well as help your brain produce the correct amount of hormone to regulate hunger. This leads to feelings of fullness and satisfaction with smaller meal sizes, lower blood sugar, and increased metabolism.
