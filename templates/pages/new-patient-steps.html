Steps to Register as a Patient

  • Step 1

    Verify Health Insurance

    We understand that checking if your health insurance covers our medical practice can be challenging. To make it easier for you, our team is here to help. You can simply give us a call at (513) 860-4600, and one of our employees will gladly assist in looking up your coverage details. Alternatively, you can check directly with your insurance provider to confirm whether our services are included in your plan. We're committed to ensuring a smooth and stress-free experience for you.

  • Step 2

    Schedule an Appointment

    We understand that scheduling an appointment can feel like a daunting task, which is why we prefer to do it over the phone to add a personal touch. Our goal is to ensure that every detail is taken care of and that you feel valued throughout the process. To schedule your appointment, please give us a call at (513) 860-4600. One of our friendly staff members will be more than happy to assist you, answer any questions, and make sure your appointment is set up just the way you need. We believe that this human connection guarantees a more satisfying experience.

  • Step 3

    Complete the Registration Form

    As a new patient, please make sure to complete the registration form before your appointment. You can download the form by clicking New Patient Registration Form. Once completed, bring the form with you when you arrive. This helps us prepare for your visit and ensures a more efficient and seamless experience. We appreciate your cooperation and look forward to serving you!

  • Step 4

    Arrive 15 minutes early

    We kindly ask that you arrive 15 minutes early for your appointment to ensure that all necessary paperwork is completed and to give you ample time to settle in. Please make your way to our office located at 4870 Wunnenberg Way, West Chester Township, OH 45069. Our team is dedicated to providing a smooth and welcoming experience from the moment you walk through our doors. We look forward to seeing you!
